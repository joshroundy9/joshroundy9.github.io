Introduction

Hi, my name is Josh Roundy, I am a Computer Science student attending the University at Albany, specializing in Data Science, and will graduate in 2025!

Beginnings

I first started software development at the early age of 11 as I, like many other kids my age, was obsessed with Minecraft. This obsession led me to want to start my own Minecraft server like many of my favorite youtubers had done themselves, but to truely stand out from the other servers you had to have custom coded plug ins. So I started watching tutorial videos on how to code with Java and how to use the required APIs which quickly led to my infatuation with software development.

Where I Am Today

After programming Minecraft plugins for 18 months straight I took an extended break from coding as I had temporarilly found other hobbies such as hiking, lifting, and sports. Come my Senior year of high school I rediscovered software development and decided to make it my major in college due to my prior knowledge and the incredible career opportunities it provides.
